# Hospital Management System

## Overview

A scalable full-stack Hospital Management System built to simulate real-world healthcare workflows through secure patient management, appointment scheduling, medical record handling, billing, and role-based administration. The platform is designed with modular architecture, clean UI/UX, and efficient database management to demonstrate enterprise-level healthcare software concepts.

---

# Features

## Authentication & Authorization

* Secure login system
* JWT/session-based authentication
* Role-based access control

  * Admin
  * Doctor
  * Receptionist

---

## Patient Management

* Add and manage patient profiles
* Maintain patient medical history
* Search and filter patient records
* Track visit history

---

## Appointment Scheduling

* Book and manage appointments
* Doctor assignment system
* Schedule conflict prevention
* Appointment status tracking

---

## Doctor Dashboard

* View assigned patients
* Add diagnosis and prescriptions
* Update treatment records
* Access patient history

---

## Billing & Payments

* Automated bill generation
* Consultation and treatment billing
* Payment tracking
* Bill history management

---

## Admin Controls

* Manage hospital staff
* Monitor appointments and records
* Database-level management capabilities
* System-wide analytics support
